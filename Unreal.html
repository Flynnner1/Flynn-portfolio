Mech Target Shooter

A first-person shooter mini-game where every hit powers up a mech animation.

Gameplay Highlights

Overview

This is a first-person shooter game I made where the goal is to shoot targets as accurately as possible. Every time I hit a target, a mech starts animating, which gives direct visual feedback and makes each hit feel rewarding. After hitting a certain amount of targets, the player wins the game.

Features

  • First-person target shooting mechanics
  • Hit detection and score/progress tracking
  • Mech animation that progresses with each successful hit
  • Win condition after reaching a specific number of targets

Technologies Used

  • Unity Engine
  • C# for gameplay scripting
  • Animation system for mech movement/sequence

What I Learned

  • Building first-person shooter controls and interactions
  • Creating reliable target hit detection
  • Triggering animations from gameplay events
  • Designing a clear gameplay loop with a satisfying win condition
