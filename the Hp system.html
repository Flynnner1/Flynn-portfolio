Health (HP) System

How the HP System Works
The HP system is the core of player survival in Arcana Rift. It's designed to be clear, responsive, and rewarding.
  • Health Bar: Your current health is always visible on the HUD via a classic red health bar, so you can assess your status at a glance.
  • Taking Damage: When an enemy hits you, your HP decreases.
  • Healing: You can restore health by buying it in one of the shops.
  • Defeat: If your HP drops to zero, you are defeated. You will respawn at the last checkpoint, but you lose some XP and coins as a penalty.
Growing Stronger
As part of the leveling system, you can buy Extra Maximum HP which costs 1 level. This allows you to withstand more damage and take on more powerful enemies as you progress through the game.
