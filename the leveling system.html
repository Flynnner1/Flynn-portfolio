Leveling System

How the Leveling System Works
The leveling system in Arcana Rift allows your character to grow stronger over time. Hereâ€™s how it works:
  • Gaining Experience (XP): You earn XP by defeating enemies. Tougher and rarer enemies grant more XP!
  • XP Bar: A dedicated XP bar on the HUD shows your current progress toward the next level, so you always see how close you are.
  • Leveling Up: When the XP bar is full, you level up! and it takes 5 more xp to level up to the next level.
  • Stat Progression: Each level up automatically increases your core stats, such as Max Health and Movement speed.
Stat Progression
For each level you have you can buy a upgrade in the shop. for some faster movement speed it will cost you 5 levels. but for 5 more hp it will cost you 1 level.
