How the Quest System Works
  • In Arcana Rift, there is a special NPC who gives you quests.
  • Every time you talk to the NPC, you'll receive a random quest to defeat a specific type of enemy.
  • The enemy type is chosen at random: Slime, Skeleton, or Zombie.
  • The required number of kills is also randomly picked (for example, between 5 and 12).
  • Once you complete the quest, return to the NPC to finish and possibly receive another one!
How it Feels in Game

This system makes each quest feel fresh and keeps players engaged. Sometimes you may get an easy task, like defeating just a few slimes, while other times you'll need to hunt down a tough band of skeletons or zombies! The randomness encourages exploration and replayability.

Example Quests
  • Defeat 8 Slimes
  • Defeat 11 Skeletons
  • Defeat 6 Zombies
(Both the type of enemy and the number needed are randomized each time you accept a new quest!)
Rewards
  • After completing your quest, talk to the NPC to receive your reward. This could be coins or XP.
