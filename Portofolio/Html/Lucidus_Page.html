Lucidus

A 2D puzzle-platformer and surviving your dreams.

Overview

Lucidus is a story-like 2D game where players control a character who lives in a normal house and does a lot of chores. After he gets tired, he goes to bed and enters a dream. The more chores he does during the day, the more time he gets in the dream. If he doesn't finish the game in his dream, he will wake up in the night to sleep paralysis, where demons will come out and attack him. To fight them back, he uses a flashlight.

Features

  • Dynamic light and monster attack mechanics
  • A 3D first-person shooter sequence
  • Atmospheric art style and sound design
  • Multiple in-game tasks

Technologies Used

  • Unity Engine
  • C# for scripting
  • Universal Render Pipeline (URP) for 2D lighting effects
  • Krita for 2D art

What I Learned

  • Advanced C# scripting for game logic
  • Implementing 2D lighting and shadow effects in Unity
  • Integrating a 2D game within a 3D world
  • Creating a cohesive player experience through art, sound, and mechanics
  • Working in a team of 5
