Enemy Variations

Arcana Rift features a diverse cast of enemies. Each enemy has its own look, abilities, and behavior, offering unique challenges to the player. Here are 25 of the enemies you'll encounter in the game:

Slime
A classic weak enemy that moves slowly and attacks by bumping into the player. Great for beginners to practice combat. It has a 91.27% chance to spawn.
Stone Slime
A sturdier version of the standard slime, with increased health. It has a 5.45% chance to spawn.
Kirby Slime
A rare, fast-moving slime with a unique appearance. It has a 2% chance to spawn.
Water Slime
A blue slime that glides around the battlefield. It has a 1.09% chance to spawn.
Gold Slime
A super rare slime that is hard to catch. Defeating it rewards extra coins. It has a 0.18% chance to spawn.
Zombie
A slow-moving undead enemy. Deals damage if it gets close and it has a 91.27% chance to spawn.
Raged Zombie
A zombie driven mad. Moves much faster and attacks more aggressively than a normal zombie. And it has a 5.45% chance to spawn.
Drowned Zombie
A waterlogged zombie that has a 2% chance to spawn.
Ash Zombie
A zombie that is completely burned and turned into ash, making it super fast and it has a 1.09% chance to spawn.
Sand Zombie
The Sand zombie is the most rare one of all the zombies by having a 0.18% chance to spawn.
Skeleton
A classic bony foe that shoots arrows from a distance. Medium health and quick movement. It has a 91.27% chance to spawn.
Armored Skeleton
A skeleton with iron armor, giving more health to a skeleton. It has a 5.45% chance to spawn.
Ice Skeleton
A skeleton imbued with ice powers. It deals more damage than a normal skeleton. It has a 2% chance to spawn.
Gold Skeleton
A rare skeleton that drops bonus gold. It has a 1.09% chance to spawn.
Skeleton General
Leads other skeletons in battle, having much more hp and damage than the rest. It has a 0.18% chance to spawn.
Demon
A demonic enemy that runs towards you with his trident. Fast and aggressive. It has a 91.27% chance to spawn.
Giant Demon
A massive demon that deals a lot of damage but is a lot slower. High health and strength. It has a 5.45% chance to spawn.
Demon Mage
A caster demon that launches various dangerous spells from afar. It has a 2% chance to spawn.
Demon Necromancer
Summons zombies to fight alongside it. It has a 1.09% chance to spawn.
Demon King
The ruler of all demons, buffs other demons. It has a 0.18% chance to spawn.

Brown Cow

Angry Cow
This cow charges at the player when he gets close. Watch out for its horns! It has a 2% chance to spawn.
Pink Cow
A cow with a unique pink color. Very rare and friendly. It has a 1.09% chance to spawn.
Gold Cow
