Spells & Abilities

Arcana Rift lets you wield a variety of spells, each with its own unique visuals and gameplay effect. Here are the core spells you can use:

Pebbles
Launches a quick barrage of 5 small rocks at your foes. Low damage but fast.
Fireball
Cast a blazing fireball that deals avarage damage. Classic and reliable!
Slash
Unleash a powerful energy slash in front of you. Deals low damage in a short time.
Laser Beam
Fires a piercing beam of magical energy in a straight line. Can hit multiple enemies at once and is great for clearing paths and deals the most damage in the game
Poison Splash
Hurls a blob of poison that splashes on impact, creating a toxic pool that damages and poisons enemies and the player over time.
All of these Spell sprites were drawn by myself using pixelart!
