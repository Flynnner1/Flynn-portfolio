Enemy Spawning System

Zone-Based Spawning
In Arcana Rift, the world is divided into different zones or biomes. Each zone has its own unique pool of enemies, meaning you'll encounter different creatures depending on where you are. This system encourages exploration, as you'll need to travel to specific areas to complete quests or hunt for rare loot.
Spawning Areas & Enemy Pools
The High Grass Fields
A lush, green area with high grass, teeming with life. You'll mostly find common creatures here.
Spawns: Slimes.
The Boneyard
An eerie field scattered with old bones, where the undead are known to rise.
Spawns: Skeletons.
The Bloodmeadow
An eerie meadow where zombies endlessly wander among the fallen.
Spawns: Zombies.
The Demonic Wastes
A corrupted land where powerful demons roam. Only for the brave!
Spawns: Demons.
The Flower Fields
A quiet place where cows roam and eat flowers. But watch out for the angry ones!
Spawns: Cows.
Spawn Rarity System
Within each zone, not all enemies are created equal. The spawning system uses a weighted chance to determine which enemy appears. While you'll frequently see common enemies, there's always a small chance for a much rarer and tougher variant to spawn, like a Gold Slime or a Demon King. Defeating these rare enemies yields better rewards!
