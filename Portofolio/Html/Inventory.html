Inventory System

How the Inventory Works:

The inventory system in Arcana Rift is designed to be intuitive and efficient for players:
  • Open/Close Inventory: Press I to open or close your inventory at any time during the game.
  • Add Items: When you collect loot or rewards, they automatically appear in the next available slot in your inventory grid.
  • Move Items: Drag and drop items between slots to organize your inventory as you wish.
  • Item Stacking: Identical items (such as potions) stack together, saving space.
  • Use Items: Right-click or select an item to use or equip it, depending on its type.
  • Hotbar Integration: Drag items to your hotbar for quick access during gameplay. The hotbar is mapped to number keys for instant use.
  • Item Tooltips: Hover over any item to see its name, description, and effects.
  • Remove Items: Drag items outside the inventory window (to a trash icon) to discard them.
This system was built in Unity using C# and supports easy future expansion for new item types and features!
